In **Headless CMS**, you can find all your storeâ€™s website URL routes and page templates: `Global Alert Page` `Page`,`Product Page`. You can also create and link custom pages to predefined templates and manage pages created directly in the source code of your storefront project.

Follow the instructions below to create and manage a page:
![Pages](https://images.ctfassets.net/alneenqid6w5/6rzTf5xTBpR3P8sUonA0Ms/859e7a998e9ebc681c8bc5e2983e1777/pages_um.png)

1. In your VTEX Admin, access **Headless CMS**.
2. Click `CREATE NEW` and select the desired page. 
3. Click <i class="fa-solid fa-circle-plus"></i> and choose a section.
![Gif - Pages](https://images.ctfassets.net/alneenqid6w5/1JOLEO3RErUiJ8AYroKwTe/d35ca90a8b65f2f436173375fee0bf9d/Ingles_gif_da_pag.gif)
4. Name the page and fill in the fields.
5. Click `Publish`. 

By clicking <i class="fa-solid fa-ellipsis-vertical"></i>, you can choose to `Duplicate` your page or `Delete` it.
