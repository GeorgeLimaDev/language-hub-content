To edit customer information you must do the following:

1. In the VTEX Admin, access **Store Settings** > **Storefront** > **Master Data**.
2. Select the **Endereços** tab. Here you will find all the users who have registered at least one address.
3. Find the customer you want.
4. In the **Ações** column of the table, click on the option **Editar**.
5. Create or edit the required information.
6. Click on **Salvar**.
7. Then click on **Voltar** to see the list of addresses again.
