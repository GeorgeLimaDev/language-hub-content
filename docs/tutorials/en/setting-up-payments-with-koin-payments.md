At VTEX, it is possible to integrate with the Koin-Payments payment provider. With this connector, your store can make sales through a credit and debit cards, PIX and BoletoParceladoKoin.

To configure Koin-Payments affiliation, follow the steps below:

1. In the VTEX Admin, go to **Store Settings** > **Payment** > **Settings**, or type **Settings** in the search bar at the top of the page.
2. In the __Gateway affiliations__ tab, click on the `+` button.
3. Click on the __Koin-Payments__ connector.
4. Fill in the __Application Key__ and __Application Token__ fields with data provided by Koin-Payments.
5. Click on `Save`.

To configure the payment methods to be processed by Koin-Payments, access [Configuring Payment Conditions](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/how-to-configure-payment-conditions--tutorials_455#).

To set special conditions on payment methods, go to [Configuring payment special conditions](https://help.vtex.com/en/tutorial/special-conditions--tutorials_456#).

After following the indicated steps, Koin-Payments connector may take up to 10 minutes to appear at your store's checkout as a payment option.
