## Summary


In the Fullcleanup admin, there is a button to reindex the products of the account. It is expected that this button will perform the action on the first attempt, however, for the action to be performed it may be necessary to click the button several times.


##

## Simulation



1. Open Fullcleanup admin - `/Admin/Site/FullCleanUp.aspx Â `
2. Click the button **Reindex Database**
3. Nothing may occur


##

## Workaround


Try to click on the bottom more times
